The Doctrine of God

God the Father - Week 7

Nature and Attributes of God

  • Love: God is love. (1 John 4:8)
  • Holiness: Be ye holy; for I am holy. (1 Peter 1:16)
  • Mercy, Compassion, and Grace: God is full of compassion, gracious, and plenteous in mercy. (Psalm 86:15, Ephesians 2:8)
  • Longsuffering: God is longsuffering. (Psalm 86:15)
  • Wrath: God is a consuming fire and is angry with the wicked. (Psalm 7:11, Romans 1:18)
  • Eternal: From everlasting to everlasting, thou art God. (Psalm 90:2, Revelation 1:8)
  • Just: A God of truth and without iniquity, just and right is he. (Deuteronomy 32:4, Romans 2:11)
  • Immutable, Unchanging: I am the LORD, I change not. (Malachi 3:6, James 1:17)
  • Omnipotent: The Lord God omnipotent reigneth. (Revelation 19:6)
  • Omnipresent: There is nowhere to flee from His presence. (Psalm 139:7-10)
  • Omniscient: Known unto God are all his works from the beginning. (Acts 15:18)

The Godhead/Trinity

  • Three that bear record in heaven: the Father, the Word, and the Holy Ghost, and these three are one. (1 John 5:7)
  • Baptizing them in the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Ghost. (Matthew 28:19)

The Seven Spirits of God

  • The Spirit of wisdom
  • The Spirit of understanding
  • The Spirit of counsel
  • The Spirit of might

  • The Spirit of the fear of the Lord

The Names of God

  • Elohim: God as the Creator. (Genesis 1:1)
  • I AM THAT I AM: God's eternal nature. (Exodus 3:14)
  • Almighty God [El Shaddai]: The all-powerful and all-sufficient One. (Genesis 17:1)
  • The LORD [Jehovah]: Our shepherd and provider. (Psalm 23:1)
  • Jealous [Jehovah Qanna]: God's demand for exclusive loyalty. (Exodus 34:14)
  • A consuming fire: God's holiness and judgment. (Deuteronomy 4:24)
  • God is our refuge and strength [El Ma'oz]: Our strong, safe place. (Psalm 46:1)
  • Immanuel: God with us. (Isaiah 7:14)
  • Lord of lords: God's supreme authority. (Deuteronomy 10:17)
  • The living God: The source of all life. (Jeremiah 10:10)
  • Jesus: The Savior. (Matthew 1:21)

The Nature of the Father

  • Gave His only begotten Son for the world. (John 3:16)
  • Sealed the Son of man, giving Him authority. (John 6:27)
  • One God and Father of all, who is above all, and through all, and in you all. (Ephesians 4:6)
  • The Father as the ultimate source of all things. (1 Corinthians 8:6)

Seven Things God Hates

  • A proud look
  • A lying tongue
  • Hands that shed innocent blood
  • An heart that deviseth wicked imaginations
  • Feet that be swift in running to mischief
  • A false witness that speaketh lies
  • And he that soweth discord among brethren
