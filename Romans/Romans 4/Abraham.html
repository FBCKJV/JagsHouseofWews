Abraham

Believed God, and It Was Counted unto Him for Righteousness
(Genesis 15:6; Romans 4:3)
Before age 75 (while in Ur of the Chaldees, Mesopotamia) The God of glory appeared to Abram and gave the first promise/call: “Get thee out of thy country, and from thy kindred, and from thy father’s house, unto a land that I will shew thee.”
(Genesis 12:1; Acts 7:2-3)
↓
Age unknown (but before 75) Terah took Abram, Sarai, and Lot from Ur toward Canaan, but they settled in Haran.
(Genesis 11:31)
↓
Age 75 After Terah’s death in Haran, God renewed the call. Abram departed Haran for Canaan with Sarai and Lot.
Now the Lord had said unto Abram, Get thee out of thy country, and from thy kindred, and from thy father’s house, unto a land that I will shew thee: And I will make of thee a great nation, and I will bless thee, and make thy name great; and thou shalt be a blessing: And I will bless them that bless thee, and curse him that curseth thee: and in thee shall all families of the earth be blessed.
(Genesis 12:1-3)
↓
Age ~75–86 Settled near the plain of Moreh and then the oaks of Mamre at Hebron, building altars to the Lord.
(Genesis 12:6-9; 13:18)
↓
Age ~80–85 Participated in the War of the Kings, rescuing Lot after defeating Chedorlaomer and his allies; met Melchizedek, king of Salem, and gave him tithes.
(Genesis 14)
↓
Age 86 Ishmael born to Hagar, Sarai’s Egyptian handmaid.
(Genesis 16:15-16)
↓
Age 99 God appeared to Abram, changed his name to Abraham (“father of many nations”) and Sarai’s to Sarah; instituted circumcision as the token of the covenant. Abraham, Ishmael (age 13), and all males in his household were circumcised.
(Genesis 17:1-27)
↓
Age 100 Isaac born to Sarah.
(Genesis 21:1-5)
↓
Age ~133 (Isaac ~33) God commanded Abraham to offer Isaac as a burnt offering on Mount Moriah. Abraham obeyed, but the angel of the Lord stopped him and provided a ram instead. God renewed the covenant promises because Abraham did not withhold his son.
(Genesis 22:1-19; Hebrews 11:17-19)
↓
Age 175 Abraham died and was buried in the cave of Machpelah near Hebron.
(Genesis 25:7-10)
