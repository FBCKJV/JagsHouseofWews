Romans 1:18-32: The Logical Descent to Judgment 📜

Paul's argument uses a strict cause-and-effect progression, typical of a **diatribe**, showing that God's judgment is a direct, logical **recompense** for human rebellion.

Part I: The Premise and Rejection (The Cause)

**IF** God’s truth is clearly seen from creation, and wrath is revealed against ungodliness,
⬇️
**THEN** they are **without excuse**, Because they knew God.
⬇️
**BUT** they rejected this knowledge: they glorified him not as God, neither were thankful.
⬇️
**RESULT:** They became vain in their imaginations, their foolish heart was darkened, and they **changed the glory** of God into idols.
⬇️
**THE ULTIMATE REJECTION:** They changed the truth of God into a lie, and worshipped and served the **creature more than the Creator**.

Part II: The Sequence of Judgment (The Effect)

For this rebellion, God delivers them to the natural and just consequences of their perversion (three "gave them up" judgments).

Judgment Logical Marker God's Action ("Gave Them Up") The Resulting Sin (Recompense of Error)
**1st Judgment** Wherefore God gave them up to **uncleanness** through the lusts of their own hearts. To dishonour their own bodies between themselves.
**2nd Judgment** For this cause God gave them up unto **vile affections**. Women and men changing the natural use into that which is against nature (Lesbianism/Homosexuality), receiving the fitting **recompense of their error**.
**3rd Judgment** And even as God gave them over to a **reprobate mind**, to do those things which are not convenient. Being filled with all unrighteousness, even though they **know the judgment of God** (that they are worthy of death), they take pleasure in them that do them.
