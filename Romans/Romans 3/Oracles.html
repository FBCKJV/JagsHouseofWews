Romans Chapter 3

The Oracles of God Prove That Jew and Gentile Alike Are Guilty Before God

Paul’s Question After Romans 2

Romans 2 just finished proving there is NO difference between Jew and Greek (Gentile).
God is no respecter of persons.
Circumcision and being under the Law profit nothing if you sin.
Not having the Law is no excuse if you do right by nature.
“What advantage then hath the Jew?
or what profit is there of circumcision?”

Paul’s Answer

Much every way!
Chiefly, because unto them were committed the oracles of God — the inspired Old Testament Scriptures.
Paul now uses those very oracles of God (the Law and the Prophets) to prove his point to both Jew and Gentile.
This silences proud Jews and shows believing Gentiles that grace for all nations was always God’s plan.

Romans 3:4 – Quoting David

“God forbid: yea, let God be true, but every man a liar…”
Direct quote from David after his sin with Bathsheba:
Psalm 51:4 – “Against thee, thee only, have I sinned… that thou mightest be justified when thou speakest, and be clear when thou judgest.”
