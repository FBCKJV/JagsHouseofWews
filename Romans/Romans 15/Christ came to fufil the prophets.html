Romans 15:8-13

Old Testament Prophecies • Gentiles United with Israel in Christ

Romans 15:8-9

Now I say that Jesus Christ was a minister of the circumcision for the truth of God, to confirm the promises made unto the fathers:

And that the Gentiles might glorify God for his mercy...

Christ came to the Jews to keep God’s promises.
He came to the Gentiles to show God’s mercy.

Four Old Testament Witnesses

1. Psalm 18:49
“Therefore will I confess to thee among the Gentiles, and sing unto thy name.”

2. Deuteronomy 32:43
“Rejoice, ye Gentiles, with his people.”

3. Psalm 117:1
“Praise the Lord, all ye Gentiles; and laud him, all ye people.”

4. Isaiah 11:10
“There shall be a root of Jesse, and he that shall rise to reign over the Gentiles; in him shall the Gentiles trust.”

The Prophecy Fulfilled

The entire Old Testament — Law, Psalms, and Prophets — foretold that Gentiles would glorify God together with Israel under the Messiah.

Jesus Christ, the Root of Jesse, now reigns over both Jew and Gentile. Both groups are called to worship the same God with one mind and one mouth (v. 6).

This is not a new plan — it is the fulfillment of God’s ancient promises.

Application & Blessing

Wherefore receive ye one another, as Christ also received us to the glory of God. (v. 7)

Now the God of hope fill you with all joy and peace in believing, that ye may abound in hope, through the power of the Holy Ghost. (v. 13)
