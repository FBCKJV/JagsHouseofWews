O wretched man that I am!
who shall deliver me from the body of this death?

(Romans 7:24)

I thank God through Jesus Christ our Lord!

So then with the mind I myself serve the law of God;
but with the flesh the law of sin.

“I myself” — the true believer, the inward man — is the real “I,”
the new creation that is the righteousness of God in Christ
(2 Corinthians 5:21; Galatians 2:20).

The flesh is not our identity — it is crucified with Christ,
dead and nailed to the cross (Romans 6:6; Colossians 2:14).

(Romans 7:25) — The door of hope that opens to the victory of chapter 8
