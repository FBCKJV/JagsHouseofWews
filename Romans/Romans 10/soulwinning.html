A Call to Soul-Winning

Romans 10:14–17 • The Chain from Sending to Salvation

“How then shall they call on him in whom they have not believed?
and how shall they believe in him of whom they have not heard?
and how shall they hear without a preacher?
And how shall they preach, except they be sent?
as it is written, How beautiful are the feet of them that preach the gospel of peace,
and bring glad tidings of good things!

…So then faith cometh by hearing, and hearing by the word of God.”

God SENDS His people ↓ Soul-winners PREACH the gospel of peace ↓ The lost HEAR the word of Christ ↓ FAITH COMETH by hearing ↓ They BELIEVE on the Lord Jesus Christ ↓ They CALL upon His name and are SAVED

Paul asks the questions backward (from calling all the way back to sending) to show every step is essential.

But the process itself flows forward: God sends → we preach → they hear → faith comes → they believe → they call → they are saved.
