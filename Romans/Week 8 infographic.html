The Doctrine of Christ: A Visual Guide

Key Truths and Names of Jesus

The Promise of Christ

  • Genesis 3:15: The first prophecy of Christ, the coming deliverer.
  • Isaiah 7:14: A prophecy of the virgin birth of Immanuel.
  • Micah 5:2: A prophecy of Christ’s birthplace and eternal nature.

Theophanies / Christophanies

  • Genesis 16:7-13: The "Angel of the LORD" is often seen as a pre-incarnate Jesus.
  • Genesis 14:18-20 & Hebrews 7:1-3: The appearance of Melchizedek is seen as a type of Christ.
  • Daniel 3:24-25: The fourth man in the fiery furnace protects His followers.

Jesus as Creator of all Things

  • John 1:3: All things were made by Him, and without Him was nothing made.
  • Colossians 1:16: All things were created by Him and for Him, and He sustains them.
  • Hebrews 1:2: By whom also the Father made the worlds.

Eternal Sonship

  • John 1:1-2: Jesus as the eternal Word, co-existent with the Father.
  • Hebrews 1:8: The Son is called "God," and His throne is eternal.
  • Proverbs 30:4: A foreshadowing of the Father and the Son in the Old Testament.

The Deity of Jesus

  • John 1:1: The Word was God.
  • Colossians 2:9: The fullness of the Godhead dwells in Him bodily.
  • Titus 2:13: He is called "the great God and our Saviour."

The Humanity of Jesus

  • Philippians 2:7: He took on the form of a servant.
  • Hebrews 2:14: He shared in humanity to conquer death.
  • 1 Timothy 2:5: He is the man Christ Jesus, the sole mediator.

Salvation in Jesus

  • Acts 4:12: There is salvation in no other name.
  • John 14:6: Jesus is the exclusive path to the Father.
  • Romans 10:9: Confess and believe to be saved.

The 7 "I Am" Statements

  • Bread of Life (John 6:35)
  • Light of the World (John 8:12)
  • Door/Gate (John 10:7)
  • Good Shepherd (John 10:11)
  • Resurrection and the Life (John 11:25)
  • Way, the Truth, and the Life (John 14:6)
  • True Vine (John 15:1)

The Names of Christ

  • The Word (John 1:1)
  • Immanuel (Isaiah 7:14)
  • The Son of God (John 1:34)
  • The Son of Man (Matthew 16:27)
  • The Lamb of God (John 1:29)
  • King of kings and Lord of lords (Revelation 19:16)
  • Alpha and Omega (Revelation 1:8)
  • The Good Shepherd (John 10:11)
